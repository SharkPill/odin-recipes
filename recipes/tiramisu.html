Tiramisu

Description

The best tiramisu recipe, inspired by the best french cookers!

Delicious but cheap and easy to make

Ingredients

  • Mascarpone
  • Coffee
  • Cookies of your choice
  • Cacao powder
  • Fruits of your choice (optional)

Steps

  1. Let the cookies soak in the coffee all night along.
  2. Then, start by cutting the fruits into medium pieces.
  3. In a dish, arrange the biscuits and cover them with a layer of mascarpone
  4. Add some fruits and start over again
  5. Finally, let it rest in your fridg, and enjoy!
