Aglio, Olio e Pepperoncino

Description

The best AOP recipe, inspired by french best cookers!

Delicious but cheap and easy to make

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Pepperoncino
  • garlic
  • Parmigiano (optional)
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta with a 1 liter of water and 10 grams of salt per 100 grams.
  2. While cooking the pasta, finely chop the garlic and the pepperoncino. Cook them in a pan with some olive oil
  3. Before the pasta is fully cooked, drain them and reserve. Keep some cooking water and add it to the pan.
  4. Add the pasta to the pan and let it rest a few minuts to finish cooking
  5. Add some pepper and maybe a little parmigiano, then enjoy!
