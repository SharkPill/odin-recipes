Burger

Description

The best burger recipe, inspired by french best cookers!

Delicious but cheap and easy to make

Ingredients

  • Steack
  • Bacon
  • Cheddar
  • Buns
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the steak with a little oil and salt. Pepper it.
  2. While cooking, prepare the sauce by adding oil to the salted egg yolk and scrambling
  3. When the steak is ready, poor the bacon into the pan with a little water
  4. Cut cheddar slices, add the sauce to our buns
  5. Finally, add your steak and the crispy bacon, and enjoy!
