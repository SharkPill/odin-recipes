Carbonara

Description

The best carbonara recipe, inspired by best french cookers!

Delicious and easy to make

Ingredients

  • Guanciale
  • Pasta
  • Parmigiano
  • Pepper
  • Eggs
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta with 1 liter of water and 10 grams of salt per 100 grams.
  2. While cooking the pasta, fry the guanciale in a pan
  3. Before the pasta is fully cooked, drain them and reserve. Keep some cooking water and add it to the pan
  4. Add an egg yolk to the pan, then the pasta and stir well
  5. Finally, add some gratted parmigiano and a lot of pepper, and enjoy!
